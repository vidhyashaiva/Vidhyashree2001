Banana Pancakes

Banana pancakes are a delicious and easy breakfast dish made with ripe bananas, flour, eggs, and milk. The mashed banana is incorporated into the pancake batter to create a light and fluffy texture.
The pancakes can be served with a variety of toppings like butter, syrup, fresh fruit, and nuts. Not only do they make a great breakfast, but they can also be enjoyed as a snack or dessert. Banana pancakes are a healthy, vegan, and gluten-free option for a comforting and flavorful meal.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Mutton Biriyani breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Gather ingredients: 2 ripe bananas, 1 cup of all-purpose flour, 2/3 cup of milk, 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda, and 2 tablespoons of butter.

step 2

Mash the bananas in a bowl. In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually mix in the milk, melted butter, and mashed bananas until the mixture is blended.

step 3

Heat a greased skillet over medium heat. Using a ladle, pour 1/4 cup of the batter onto the skillet. Cook until the edges start to become golden brown. Flip the pancake and cook until both sides are golden brown. Serve with your favorite topping. Enjoy!

ingredients
